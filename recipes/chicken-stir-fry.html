Chicken Stir Fry

Description

Very easy and very tasty chicken stiry fry recipe with a sweet honey soy sauce!

Stiry Fry Ingredients

  • 1 lb boneless, skinless, chicen breast (cut into small cubes)
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 cups of broccoli florets
  • 1/2 yellow and 1/2 red bell peppers (chopped)
  • 1/2 cup baby carrots (sliecd)
  • 2 teaspoons of minced ginger
  • 2 minced garlic cloves

Sauce Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon of corn starch
  • 2 tablespoons of cold water
  • 1/4 cup of low sodium chicken broth
  • 3 tablespoons of low sodium soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup of honey
  • 1 tablespoon of toasted sesame oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon of crushed red pepper flakes (adds a kick)

Instructions

  1. In a medium size bowl, whisk corn starch and water. Once combined, add remaining ingredients: chicken broth, toasted sesame oil, honey, soy sauce, red pepper flakes).
  2. Add 1 tablespoon of olive oil to large pan on medium high heat.
  3. Add chicken and season with salt and pepper. Cook 3-5 minutes (or until cooked through)
  4. Remove chicken from pan and reduce heat to medium while adding tablespoon of olive oil.
  5. Add broccoli, bell pepper, and carrots and cook (don't forget to stir) just until crispy. Add ginger and garlic and cook for an additional minute.
  6. Add chicken back into the pan and mix to combine.
  7. Whisk stir fry sauce and pour into pan with chicken and vegetable. Stir gently to combine.
  8. Stirring occasionally and bring to a boil. Let boil for one minute. Then serve!
