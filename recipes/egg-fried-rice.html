Egg Fried Rice

Description

Homemade Egg Fried Rice!

This recipe is super easy to make and doesn't require many ingredients. If you love to eat egg fried rice whenever you can, then this recipe is great for you!

Ingredients

  • 3 tablesoon of butter, divided
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 medium carrots (peeled and diced"
  • 1 small white onion (diced)
  • 1/2 cup of frozen peas
  • 3 cloves of garlic (minced)
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 4 cups of cooked and chilled rice (I like jasmine rice)
  • 3-4 tablespoons of soy sauce (add more if you'd like)
  • 1/2 teaspoon of toasted sesame oil

Instructions

  1. Heat 1/2 tablespoon of butter in large pan over medium-high heat until it melts. Whisk and add the eggs until scrambled.
  2. Transfer the eggs to a separate plate or bowl.
  3. Add 1 tablespoon of butter to the pan and wait until it's melted.
  4. Add the carrots, onion, peas, and garlic. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Cook for about 5 minutes or until the onion and carrots are soft.
  6. Increase heat and add in 1 1/2 tablespoons of butter.
  7. Once melted, add rice, green onions, and soy sauce. Stir until it's all combined.
  8. Continue cooking and stiring for about 3 minutes and then add back in the eggs.
  9. Once all is combined, remove from heat and stir in the toasted sesame oil.
  10. Let rest for a couple minutes and serve!
