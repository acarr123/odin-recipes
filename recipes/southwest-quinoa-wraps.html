Southwest Quinoa Wraps

Description

Tasty, simple, easy to make wraps that pack a protein a punch!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cooked quinoa in vegetable broth
  • 15 oz can of black beans rinsed and drained
  • 1 cup of corn
  • 2/3 cups of salsa
  • 1/4 cup of chopped cilantro
  • 1 packet of taco seasoning
  • Tortillas of your choice

Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl (can leave out half the taco seasoning to try first).
  2. Mix the ingredients together.
  3. Taste to see if rest of taco seasoning is needed.
  4. Spread on tortillas, wrap, and serve!
